I am a health data scientist and epidemiologist with a rare combination: graduate training in causal inference methodology from Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health, real-world government surveillance experience at the Maryland Department of Health, and hands-on production analytics across institutional and healthcare environments in Canada and internationally.
